My Story

I have been cleaning for 14 years. In a hectic and time management environment. I have loved my job. But I had an opportunity to do something new. I left that job and thought what can I do to make my life worth something meaningful to me.

I had some really close friends who thought starting my own business would be really good for me. I didn't think too much of it until one day I had an opportunity to go backwards. And it hit me that I do love to clean and I do love to make people happy. And less stressed out.

I can do that I can help people in a meaningful way more than I thought or even expected. I see detailing the tidiest of things fun to me. I enjoy it. So here I am now starting a new journey and meeting new people making them happy so here I am starting up a new business never in my life I would never think was possible.

Scared as hell. But know that I can and will be able to get Jordan's Dream Cleaning off the ground.
